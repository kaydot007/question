General Questions and Answers

What is the capital city of Nigeria?

Abuja, city, capital of Nigeria. It lies in the central part of Nigeria, in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT; created 1976).

Who is the richest man in the world?

The cofounder of Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Bill Gates has a net worth of $113 billion.

Highest mountain in the world?

The highest mountain in the world is Mount Everest, sitting pretty at 8,848m in the Himalayas in Nepal.
